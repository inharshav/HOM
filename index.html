I’m interested, what now??

Seize the opportunity and submit your online application here!

Registration link button

When is the deadline to apply?

We accept applications until 25th December,2021/31st December, 2021

Keep an eye out for the confirmation email upon your successful submission; if you do not receive it, it might have landed in your junk/spam folder.

What are the eligibility criteria?

You have to meet the following criteria to be eligible for consideration: • A citizen (passport holder) of either Bangladesh, India or Pakistan (either living in or outside these countries) • A young professional, entrepreneur or student

• Excellent command of English (written and verbal) • Experiences in Hackathons/Innovations or similar human-centred design events, either as participant, resource person or organiser. • Available to actively participate in all sessions in all phases.

How many hackathon teams will be selected?

(The number of teams....)

Hackathon Teams will be selected from each track.

I do not have experience in hackathons/ facilitation /sustainable urbanization can I still apply

Yes. While we seek individuals with a certain level of knowledge and experience in both the relevant hard and soft skills, we are open to accepting those with less experience, provided they demonstrate strong motivation.

The training phase for Hackathon Team Leaders has components to introduce these skills and guidance will be offered throughout the programme.

What does the programme look like?

Please refer to the event flow which we included along with this website and feel free to reach out to our coordinators anytime.

Will there be a technical team supporting the hackathon team leaders?

Yes, the organisers will engage technical support teams for the Hackathon teams, coming from our local partner universities.

What is the maximum and minimum limit of participants for a team?

Four.

A group minimum of 2 and maximum of 4 participants are granted.

Can I work on more than one track?

No

A Hackathon Team will only work to solve 1 problem statement from one track.

. Is there a participation fee?

There are no participation or registration fees. This is a fully funded event, including the issuance of certificates.
